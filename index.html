Section 07

Reflection

"Communication and clarity are as important as technical ability — and I learned that the hard way."

Throughout my four years at VIT, I focused heavily on building technical depth — algorithms, systems, full-stack development. But it was only when I started working on real projects and the internship that I realised how much the ability to explain, document, and present your work shapes how it is received. A well-built system that cannot be communicated clearly loses much of its value.

This portfolio reflects not just what I have built, but how I have grown in presenting and contextualising that work. I now approach every project with both the engineer's question — does this work? — and the communicator's question — does this make sense to someone else?

— Roopchandar A  ·  Final Year, VIT Vellore  ·  2026
